About VinnovateIT

VinnovateIT is an OFFICIAL innovation and incubation lab of SITE School.
VinnovateIT helps its members to develop their projects both technically and as budding entrepreneurs. It is a platform for the students to come up with innovative ideas where one can work on their areas of interest and also provides an atmosphere of collaborative learning in those areas.

VinHack

Our hackathon focuses on social causes and problems. Each team will be asked to ponder over the given problem statements and develop ideas and implement them through software or both software and hardware to solve the problem. The teams with the best ideas and implementations would win.
